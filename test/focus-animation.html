Interactive Focus Animation Demo
Select an item from the dropdown, choose an easing function, set the duration, and click "Focus Selected Item" to see smooth animations. The timeline will automatically scroll vertically if the item is outside the visible area.

Zoom Behavior:
  • Auto (Smart): Zooms to large items, slides small items into view
  • Always Zoom: Always zooms to show the item's full time range
  • Never Zoom: Keeps current zoom level, just pans to the item
