Interactive Keypress Timeline
Press and hold any key on your keyboard to see it visualized on the timeline:
  • Short press: Creates a point-in-time (PIT) item
  • Long press: Creates a range item that updates in real-time as you hold the key
  • Multiple keys: Each key creates its own timeline event
The timeline updates continuously while you hold a key, showing the duration of the press.
